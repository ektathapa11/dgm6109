Our Story and Our Mission

Our Brand

We are a contemporary clothing brand rooted in the rich cultural heritage of Nepal, bringing traditional craftsmanship into the evolving fashion culture of Canada.

Our Mission

Our mission is to preserve and elevate the artistry of traditional Nepalese craftsmanship by bringing it into a modern, global context. We strive to create thoughtfully designed clothing that honors cultural heritage while seamlessly fitting into contemporary fashion.

Return and Exchange Polices

We accept returns and exchanges within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unused, and in their original condition with tags attached.

Exchanges are subject to availability. If unavailable, a refund or store credit will be issued.

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is defective or incorrect.

To request a return or exchange, please contact us with your order details.

Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days after the item is received and inspected.
