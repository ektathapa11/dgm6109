How Can We Help You Today?

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Orders & Products

    • Our products are made using high-quality, carefully selected materials that reflect traditional Nepalese craftsmanship. Many of our pieces are handmade by skilled artisans, ensuring each item carries unique detail and authenticity. While we aim to restock popular items, availability may vary due to the handcrafted nature of our collections. We make every effort to display product colors as accurately as possible, though slight variations may occur due to lighting and screen settings.

  • Shipping & Delivery

    • We offer both domestic and international shipping to bring our products to a wider audience. Delivery times may vary depending on location, but orders are typically processed and shipped within a few business days. Once your order has been dispatched, tracking information will be provided so you can follow its journey. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and selected delivery method.

  • Returns & Exchanges

    • We accept returns and exchanges within 14 days of delivery, provided items are unworn, unused, and in their original condition. Exchanges are subject to availability, and if the requested item is not in stock, a refund or store credit will be offered. To request a return or exchange, simply contact us with your order details. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item received is defective or incorrect.

  • Sizing & Fit

    • We provide sizing guidance to help you choose the best fit for your needs. Our pieces are generally designed to be true to size, though slight variations may occur depending on the style and fabric. We recommend referring to our size guide for accurate measurements. At this time, custom sizing may be limited, but we continuously work towards offering more inclusive options.

  • Brand & Authenticity

    • Our products are made in Nepal in collaboration with local artisans who bring generations of skill and tradition to each piece. We are committed to preserving authentic craftsmanship by working directly with makers and maintaining close relationships throughout the production process. Every item reflects cultural heritage, thoughtful design, and ethical sourcing practices. By supporting these artisans, we aim to celebrate Nepalese tradition while introducing it to a broader, modern audience.
