Lab 9 - Smartphone Usage Data Visualizations

This assignment explores relationships in my personal smartphone usage data using two D3.js scatterplot visualizations. Both plots investigate how screen time relates to mental overload and notification activity throughout the day. Additional variables such as app switches and time of day are included to explore deeper patterns in the data.
